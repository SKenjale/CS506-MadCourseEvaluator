Welcome to MadCourseEvaluator!


Welcome to MadCourseEvaluator where you can get it all! As a student or guest, MadCourseEvaluator offers you a chance
to browse through UW-Madison course information, search for grade distribution to specific courses and
search Professors and learn about their grade distributions and average GPA.


Why use MadCourseEvaluator??


MadCourseEvaluator offers students a chance to view professor ratings and course information on one location.
It is a combination of MadGrades and RateMyProfessor without having to go to a different website
to add class/professor ratings or add comments about a class you might have already taken.
